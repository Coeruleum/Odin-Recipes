Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made by wide, flat pasta sheets with a rich meat sauce (ragù), creamy béchamel, and cheese, then baked until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients needed:

  • Lasagna noodles (12 sheets)
  • Ground beef (500g)
  • Tomato sauce (2 cups)
  • Onion (1, finely chopped)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Olive oil (2 tbsp)
  • Ricotta cheese (1 1/2 cups)
  • Mozzarella cheese (2 cups, shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup, grated)
  • Béchamel sauce (2 cups)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • Sauté onions and garlic, brown ground beef, add tomato sauce, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the béchamel sauce:
    • Make a roux with butter and flour, whisk in milk, and cook until thickened, seasoning with salt and pepper..
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles:
    • Boil lasagna sheets in salted water until al dente, drain, and set aside..
  4. Preheat the oven:
    • Set your oven to 375°F (190°C) while you assemble the lasagna..
  5. Assemble the lasagna layers:
    • Layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, béchamel, mozzarella, and Parmesan in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers:
    • Continue layering until all ingredients are used, finishing with a top layer of cheese.
  7. Bake the lasagna:
    • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  8. Rest and serve:
    • Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing, garnish with basil if desired, and serve.
